About this tracker

This dashboard tests a simple question against the data: has the topical range of Joe Rogan Experience guests changed over time? Every numbered episode from #1000 (August 2017) onward is assigned one canonical topic tag based on its primary guest's main public domain. The charts below show how episode volume, YouTube viewership, and month-by-month topic diversity have moved since then — the data is presented as-is, for the reader to judge.

Monthly views & topic diversity

Left axis: total YouTube views of episodes aired that month. Right axis: topic diversity score — distinct topic tags that month as a % of all 20 possible tags. Dashed line: diversity mean.
